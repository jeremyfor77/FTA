Grown-Ups' Corner

The parent part, plainly

Real 1:6 scale

Everything is sized for 11½″ fashion dolls. Houses ship as flat panel kits with printed snap connectors — no tools, no glue. Furniture and custom pieces are printed to order.

Grown-ups do checkout

Kids design; adults order. Accounts, payments, and any uploaded photos are handled by a parent or guardian. We don't collect personal information from children.

Safety first

Printed parts are for ages 6 and up — small pieces are not for children under 3. Every part ships smoothed and inspected by hand.

Dollhouse Studio is an independent workshop and is not affiliated with, or endorsed by, Mattel, Inc. We just really love pink.
